I am from a small town called Harpswell, a peninsula on the coast of Maine. After spending my entire life in Harpswell, I began attending Wheaton College in Norton, MA in the fall of 2011. When I arrived at Wheaton, I knew I wanted to study music, but that I wanted to find a major that I was passionate about and would enjoy making a career out of. After my freshman year, I discovered computer science and declared a combined major of mathematics and computer science.
